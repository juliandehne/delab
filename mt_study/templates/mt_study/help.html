Moderation Strategies

Emotion Control

Emotion control involves encouraging respectful and positive interactions while discouraging hostile or offensive behavior.

Example:

"Please remember to maintain a respectful tone in your comments."
"Calm down! Insulting people does not help anyone... "

Norm Control

Norm control refers to establishing and enforcing the community guidelines and rules to maintain a healthy and inclusive discussion environment.

Example:

"One should refrain from using offensive language towards minorities."
"It is not ok to frame sex workers just because of their profession."

Elaboration Support

Elaboration support involves encouraging participants to provide more detailed explanations or reasoning for their opinions or claims.

Example:

"Could you please elaborate on your point about XYZ? It would help us understand your perspective better."
"When you are saying XYZ, what is your assumption?"

Consensus Seeking

Consensus seeking aims to promote understanding and collaboration by encouraging participants to consider and respect each other's viewpoints.

Example:

"You guys should consider each other's perspectives and try to find common ground."
"You guys should listen to each other more."
(Real) Example: Somehow it always seems to be all or nothing on reddit. Either keep up the empire or abandon all international commitments. Is there nothing in between?

Agenda Control

Agenda control involves guiding the discussion back to the main topic and preventing conversations from going off on unrelated tangents.

Example:

"Please stay on the topic at hand to ensure a focused discussion!"
"Please don't switch the topic or use whataboutism!"
